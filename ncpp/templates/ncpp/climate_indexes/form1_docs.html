About These Data

The Maurer et al. data set contains daily air temperature (daily mean, maximum, minimum temperature), precipitation and wind data at a 1/8 degree spatial resolution (approx. 12 km) for a 50 year period (1950-1999). The spatial extent of the data set covers all of the conterminous United States plus a bounding area that covers parts of Canada and Mexico
(specifically latitudes 25-53 North and longitudes 67-125 West).

 

Maurer 2002 Maurer, E.P., A.W. Wood, J.C. Adam, D.P. Lettenmaier, and B. Nijssen, 2002, A Long-Term Hydrologically-Based Data Set of Land Surface Fluxes and States for the Conterminous United States, J. Climate 15(22), 3237-3251.
